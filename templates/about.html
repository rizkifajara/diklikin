About Us

Di Klik In

The development of e-commerce in Indonesia has changed some consumer behavior such as shopping habits in shopping centers or stores which are now starting to switch to using online media. As long as they are connected to the internet, consumers do not have to go to shops or shopping places to get the goods or services they want. For this reason, Di Klik In is here to make it easier for online businesses to expand their scope without being limited by geographic area.

Di Klik In is an e-commerce platform created by the nation's children aimed at improving the ability of its developers to implement software designs and fulfill the project of Workshop on Implementing Software Design class.
